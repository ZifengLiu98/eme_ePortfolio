The purpose of this ePortfolio is to showcase the projects that I have developed during my Designing Integrated Media Environments class.


I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. in Educational Technology at the College of Education, University of Florida. I hold a Master's degree in Computer Software and Theories from Beijing Normal University. My research passions lie at the fascinating intersection of educational data mining and the application of artificial intelligence in education. I am dedicated to exploring how data-driven insights and AI can enhance student engagement and improve learning outcomes, ultimately making education more personalized and impactful.

For this animation, I designed each scene in PowerPoint using its built-in 3D models for the Earth and Moon, positioning them to represent the orbital layout. I applied a rotation animation to the Moon and synchronized it with a semi-transparent blue ellipse around the Earth, which simulates the tidal effect.
